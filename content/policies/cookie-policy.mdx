# Cookie Policy
_Last updated: October 5, 2025_

## Introduction
This Cookie Policy explains how Originz ("we", "our", "us") uses cookies and similar technologies to improve your experience on **https://originz.vercel.app**.

## What Are Cookies?
Cookies are small files stored on your device to help websites function efficiently and analyze usage.

## Types of Cookies We Use
- **Essential Cookies:** Required for website functionality (e.g., checkout, login).  
- **Performance Cookies:** Help us understand website performance.  
- **Analytics Cookies:** Collect anonymous usage statistics.  
- **Marketing Cookies:** Used only with your consent for remarketing or personalized ads.

## Cookie Consent
When you first visit our site, you are shown a cookie banner where you can accept or adjust your preferences. You can modify these settings anytime in your browser.

## Managing Cookies
You can delete or disable cookies in your browser settings. Please note that some features may not function properly if cookies are disabled.

## Updates
We may update this Cookie Policy periodically. Any updates will appear on this page with a new “Last updated” date.
